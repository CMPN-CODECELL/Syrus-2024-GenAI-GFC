What is ElectraPlanner???

The project predicts future electric vehicle (EV) demand based on population, purchasing power, existing users, and incentives. Recognizing current infrastructure's insufficiency, it proposes strategic charging point placement (parking lots, stations, businesses) and continued incentives (tax credits, infrastructure support, preferential access) to build a sustainable, EV-ready future.

Data Driven Policy Making

Utilizing data on population dynamics, purchasing power, existing EV adoption rates, and incentive structures, our project predicts future electric vehicle demand. This comprehensive analysis forms the foundation for strategic placement of charging points in key areas, addressing current infrastructure gaps.

Localized Demand Prediction

By harnessing localized data on demographics, economic factors, and existing EV usage patterns, our project accurately predicts demand for electric vehicles within specific regions. This granular approach enables targeted strategies for charging point placement, tailored to meet the unique needs of each locality.

Services

Report Generation

Our report generator provides concise insights on EV demand projections, charging point placement, and incentive strategies through automated processes. Customizable templates and real-time updates streamline decision-making for stakeholders in building a sustainable EV future.

Assistance

Our report generator, paired with a fine-tuned chatbot assistant, provides concise insights on EV demand, infrastructure, and incentives. Tailored templates and real-time updates facilitate decision-making for stakeholders, fostering a sustainable EV future with seamless user support.

Map

The map showcases potential sites for EV charging stations. It serves as a planning tool, aiding in the identification of high-demand areas based on factors like population density and proximity to key destinations. This visualization facilitates collaborative decision-making among stakeholders and ensures that charging points are strategically placed to enhance accessibility and meet the evolving needs of the community.
